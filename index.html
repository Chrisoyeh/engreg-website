Nursery & Primary School Curriculum
Nurturing Young Minds (Ages 2 - 10)

Our early childhood curriculum uses cognitive play and visual tools to prompt quick discovery. We cover:

  • Early literacy, writing, and phonics programs.
  • Practical life exercises and sensory motor integration.
  • Elementary mathematics, science experiments, and nature studies.
  • Cultural arts, music, foreign language foundation, and physical education.
  • Moral instruction, empathy building, and values integration.

We ensure a low student-to-teacher ratio for dedicated focus on each child.

High School Program
Building Future Leaders (Ages 11 - 17)

Our secondary curriculum is structured to prepare students for college and technical leadership. Key subjects & streams include:

  • Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Further Mathematics.
  • Arts & Humanities: Literature, History, Government, Civic Education.
  • ICT & STEAM: Coding (Python/HTML), Robotics, Digital Design, and AI introductions.
  • Leadership & College Prep: SAT/ACT coaching, public speaking, entrepreneurship.

Students have access to standard research labs, coding workshops, and dynamic sports clubs.
