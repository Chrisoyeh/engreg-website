Frequently Asked Questions

For Nursery admission, children should be at least 3 years old by September of the entry year. For Primary School, the minimum age is 5 years, and for High School (JSS1), candidates should be at least 10 years old.

Application forms can be obtained online via our portal (click 'Apply Online' in the Admission menu) or purchased directly from the school administration office at our Somolu campus.

Yes, entrance examinations and interactive assessments are conducted for candidates seeking admission into our primary and secondary sections to assess their academic readiness and placement grade.

Required documents include a photocopy of the child's birth certificate, recent academic reports/transfer certificates from their previous school, two passport-sized photographs, and a completed medical fitness form.

We offer a rich, integrated curriculum that blends the Nigerian National Curriculum with modern global learning standards, focusing on STEM, character development, and practical leadership.

Yes, we provide a wide range of co-curricular activities, including sports (football, basketball, athletics), music and drama clubs, coding and robotics clubs, and debate societies.

Yes, the school operates safe and air-conditioned school buses that cover designated routes across Somolu and surrounding areas in Lagos.

School fees can be paid via bank transfer, direct bank deposit, or through our secure online payment portal. Cash payments are not accepted at the school office for security reasons.

Yes, transfer students are admitted into non-examination classes (subject to availability of space in the requested class and successful completion of our entrance evaluation).

Absolutely! We welcome prospective parents to tour our campus and meet our team. You can schedule a school tour by contacting our admin office or visiting during our open days.
