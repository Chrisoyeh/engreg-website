Our Vision, Mission, and Aim

Guided by purpose and driven by excellence, our vision, mission, and aims define the foundation upon which Engreg Schools nurtures future leaders and responsible global citizens.

Our Core Purpose

Our Core Purpose

Our commitment to educational excellence is guided by a clear vision, a purposeful mission, and a strong institutional aim.

Our Vision

"To have a community of pupils/students and staff with abilities, character to compete and add value to the global world."

Our Mission

  1. I. To produce young men and women with Intellect, Strength, Identity, and Character (I.S.I.C).
  2. II. To discover and explore the world around them with the ability to identify and propose solutions.

Our Aim

"To provide qualitative education in a homely and exciting environment."
