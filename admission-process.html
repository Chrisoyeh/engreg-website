Admission Process

"Our admission process is simple, transparent, and designed to help every prospective pupil and student begin their journey at Engreg Schools with ease."

Step-by-Step Admission Guide

1

Obtain the Application Form

Application forms can be obtained directly from the school administrative office or by completing our secure Online Application Form.

Application Fee: ₦15,000 For online applications, payment must be completed successfully before the admission form is displayed.
2

Submit Required Documents

The candidate's parent or guardian must submit the necessary documents to the Admission Office to register the student.

Required Documents
  • Two (2) Recent Passport Photographs
  • Original Birth Certificate (for sighting)
  • Recent Academic Result from former school
  • School Testimonial (if applicable)
3

Write the Entrance Examination

All prospective pupils (from primary classes) and high school students must sit for our competitive entrance assessment.

Entrance Exam Syllabus
English Language, Mathematics, General Paper
1 Hour / Subject
4

Receive Admission Result

Entrance assessment results will be processed and published online. Successful candidates will have their names published on the school's official Successful Candidate List.

You may also visit the school administrative board to confirm your child's admission status.

5

School Fees Payment

Upon receiving the official admission letter, parent/guardian should proceed with the payment of school fees and other initial charges.

Official Account Details

Payments must be made directly to the bank account details supplied by the Admissions Office. Confirm account numbers with school admin before making bank transfers.

Complete your admission application online quickly, securely, and conveniently from anywhere.
