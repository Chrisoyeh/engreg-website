A Welcome Message From The Proprietress

Welcome to Engreg Schools, where excellence, character, and innovation come together to nurture future leaders.

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Students,

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Engreg Schools, a place where academic excellence, discipline, creativity, and leadership are cultivated daily.

At Engreg Schools, we are committed to providing a nurturing environment that empowers learners to reach their fullest potential. Our dedicated staff, modern learning facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that every child receives quality education and holistic development.

We believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Therefore, we encourage critical thinking, moral values, innovation, teamwork, and community service, preparing our students for success in an ever-changing world.

As partners in your child's educational journey, we value the trust you place in us and remain committed to helping every learner achieve excellence academically, socially, and morally.

Thank you for choosing Engreg Schools.

Mrs. E.N. Orekie

Proprietress, Engreg Schools

"Education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge; it is the foundation upon which character, leadership, and lifelong success are built."

— Proprietress
