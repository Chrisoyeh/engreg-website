Co-Curricular Activities

"Developing well-rounded learners through leadership, creativity, sportsmanship, innovation, and practical experiences beyond the classroom."

Beyond The Classroom

"At Engreg Schools, learning extends beyond the classroom. Our co-curricular programmes provide opportunities for students to discover their talents, build leadership skills, develop teamwork, and foster creativity while preparing them for future success."

Student Engagement

Clubs & Societies

Clubs and societies help students develop leadership, communication, teamwork, and practical skills. We encourage active participation to prepare students for real-world collaboration and future STEM fields.

JETS Club

Junior Engineers, Technicians, and Scientists club encourages logical STEM thinking and early innovation.

  • Encourages scientific thinking & innovation.
  • Develops problem-solving & practical skills.
  • Prepares students for STEM careers.

Press Club

Develops communication, writing, and journalism skills, keeping the school community well-informed.

  • Develops communication & journalism skills.
  • Encourages creativity & critical thinking.
  • Enhances public speaking & writing.

Art Club

Fosters creative self-expression, imagination, and artistic talents through diverse visual media.

  • Encourages creativity & self-expression.
  • Develops artistic and design talents.
  • Promotes innovation & imagination.

Debate & Literary

Builds confidence and command of language through structured debates, speech-making, and analysis.

  • Builds confidence & communication skills.
  • Encourages deep analytical thinking.
  • Develops public leadership abilities.

Drama & Cultural

Celebrates performance, drama, poetry, and regional traditions, building stage presence and cultural pride.

  • Promotes cultural awareness & creativity.

  • Encourages team coordination & confidence.
Physical Fitness

Sports & Physical Development

We believe physical education and competitive play are essential components of holistic development. Sports teach discipline, build resilience, and encourage healthy, active lifestyles.

How Sports Benefit Our Students:
  • Build Discipline: Enhances focus, time management, and commitment.
  • Develop Teamwork: Nurtures collaborative and cooperative efforts.
  • Improve Fitness: Promotes stamina, motor control, and coordination.
  • Foster Leadership: Teaches game strategy and team captaincy.
  • Healthy Lifestyle: Instills active habits and athletic interests.
