Welcome Address from the Head of School

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Students,

It is with immense pleasure that I welcome you to Engreg High School. Our high school section represents the culmination of academic preparation, personal growth, and leadership training for our students.

At Engreg High School, we focus on driving deep academic excellence, leadership development, character formation, and technological innovation. We aim to equip our young men and women with the capacity to think critically, communicate effectively, and act with integrity in a fast-paced global landscape.

Our program is designed to guide students through the transition from dependent learners to independent, analytical thinkers. We prepare them rigorously for national and international examinations, higher institutions of learning, and future careers. With our state-of-the-art STEM laboratories, ICT facilities, and creative platforms, we unlock the full creative and analytical potential of each individual.

Discipline, leadership, and mutual respect are foundational elements of our student culture. We look forward to working closely with you to guide our students towards becoming responsible global citizens and stellar future leaders.

Head of School
